Grooveshark Lyrics

When listening to Grooveshark music, simply click this bookmarklet to turn Grooveshark Lyrics on!

Drag the button below and drop it into your bookmarks.

Grooveshark Lyrics

Don't forget to open the Grooveshark website before clicking the bookmark you just created.
